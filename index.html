Who am I?

Hello there. I'm Bryan Onwas. I'm currently a freelance front-end web developer who is passionate in learning new technologies in order to solve more problems and bring more value to the industry while working on building bigger ideas and solutions. Since the beginning of my path as a front-end developer, I've done a lot of personal projects during my time as a freelancer. Projects that were challenging made me learn different frameworks and languages and allow me to grow as a developer. I'm quite confident, curious person and I'm willing to spend some extra hours on learning new things! Being a front-end developer is also my hobby as I like to develop and simulate fictional company websites as it was real.

My thought process when developing a website:

  • Step 1

    Plan

    Who's the target audience? What type of business and website? Which web stack will be used? What about cross-compatabilty?

  • Step 2

    Design

    I start designing the website based on the planning.

  • Step 3

    Execute

    I start coding the website. What I design is what I build.

  • Step 4

    Test

    I check for bugs, cross-compatabilty, and UI/UX design.

  • Step 5

    Deploy

    I pushed my code into github and deploy the website. When working with other developers, I instead will push and send a pull request.

  • Step 6

    Repeat

    I rinse and repeat the process as I add more features and content on the website.
